Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

At GameHub Pro, we are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to enhance your gaming experience:

  • Game play history and preferences
  • Browser and device information
  • Optional game suggestions or feedback

We do NOT collect:

  • Personally identifiable information without consent
  • Credit card or payment details
  • Sensitive personal data

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and improve gaming services
  • Personalize your gaming experience
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Respond to user suggestions and support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Data is stored locally in your browser
  • We use encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Data is anonymized and aggregated

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use local storage and minimal browser cookies to:

  • Remember game preferences
  • Improve user interface
  • Analyze platform performance

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect anonymous usage data, such as:

  • Game hosting platforms
  • Performance analytics tools
  • Content delivery networks

These services adhere to strict privacy guidelines.

6. User Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Clear game history
  • Reset all settings
  • Enable/disable data collection
  • Use incognito mode

7. Children's Privacy

GameHub Pro is designed for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes.
